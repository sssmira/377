A little About Us

Hi! We are a team of 5 University of Maryland students looking to make readers' experience more enjoyable. We realized that many readers oftentimes are faced with the mental roadblock of figuring out what book they should read next. Books are a large commitment of time, so readers want to find a book they know they will enjoy reading. Because of this we set out to create a website that would allow users to recieve book recommendations based on books they have previously enjoyed. We're serious about books, so we made a better way to find books!
